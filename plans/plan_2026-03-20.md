### Workout Plan

**Warm-up (10 minutes)**

* Light cardio: 5 minutes of walking in place or jogging in place
* Dynamic stretching: leg swings, arm circles, hip circles

**Resistance Training (30 minutes)**

* **Glute Bridges (3 sets of 12 reps)**
	+ Lie on your back with knees bent and feet flat on the ground
	+ Slowly lift your hips up towards the ceiling, squeezing your glutes at the top
	+ Lower your hips back down to the starting position
* **Resistance Band Rows (3 sets of 12 reps)**
	+ Hold the resistance band with both hands and stand with your feet shoulder-width apart
	+ Keeping your back straight, pull the band towards your chest
	+ Release the band back to the starting position
* **Wall Squats (3 sets of 12 reps)**
	+ Stand with your back against a wall and your feet shoulder-width apart
	+ Slowly slide your back down the wall, keeping your knees bent at a 90-degree angle
	+ Hold for a count of 10, then slowly stand up

**Cool-down (5 minutes)**

* Static stretching: focus on your glutes, hamstrings, and lower back
